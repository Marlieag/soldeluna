Our Services

All services are tailored to your needs and available by appointment only.


Deep Tissue/Trigger Point

A focused massage designed to release chronic muscle tension and knots. Deep tissue techniques combined with trigger point therapy help relieve pain, improve mobility, and target specific problem areas.

Available in: 60 | 90 minutes


Swedish massage

This calming, full-body massage uses flowing strokes to promote relaxation, improve circulation, and ease mental stress. It is perfect for those new to massage or seeking light to medium pressure.

Available in: 60 | 90 minutes


Prenatal Massage

This is a supportive, gentle massage for expectant mothers. It helps relieve discomfort, reduce swelling, and nurture emotional balance. It is safe for all stages of pregnancy.

Available in: 60 | 90 minutes


Chair Massage

A quick, clothed massage session that targets tension in the neck, shoulders, and back; ideal for workplaces, events, or anyone short on time.

Available in: 15 | 30 minutes


Lymphatic Drainage

A gentle, rhythmic technique that supports your body’s natural detox and immune function. Ideal for post-surgical care, swelling, or general wellness.

Available in: 60 | 90 minutes


Hot Stone Massage

A massage that combines traditional massage techniques with heated stones that promote relaxation and ease muscle tension.

Available in: 60 | 90 minutes

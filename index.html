Our Services

🎯 Trigger Point Therapy

Focused pressure on specific points of tension (“knots”) to relieve pain and restore movement. Often integrated into therapeutic sessions or requested as a standalone treatment.
Available in: 60 | 90 minutes

🪑 Chair Massage

Quick, clothed sessions that target tension in the neck, shoulders, and back- ideal for workplaces, events, or anyone short on time.
Available in: 15 | 30 minutes

🌸 Prenatal Massage

Supportive, gentle massage for expecting mothers. Helps relieve discomfort, reduce swelling, and nurture emotional balance. Safe for all stages of pregnancy.
Available in: 60 | 90 minutes

🧘🏻‍♂️ Swedish massage

A calming, full body massage using flowing strokesto promote relaxation, improve circulation, and ease mental stress. Perfect for those new to massage or seeking light to medium pressure.
Available in: 60 | 90 minutes

💧 Lyphatic Drainage

A gentle, rhythmic technique that supports your body’s natural detox and immune function. Ideal for post-surgical care, swelling or general wellness.
Available in: 60 | 90 minutes

🌀 Deep Tissue Massage

Focused work to address chronic muscle tension, restricted mobility, and postural imbalances, ideal for athletes or anyone needing structural relief.
Available in: 60 | 90 minutes
